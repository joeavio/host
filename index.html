Release Date of November 7, 2018

""'Baby' and 'Fall Into Pieces' were written within several weeks of each other. I had just moved to Los Angeles, and was trying to reconcile with what I’d left behind in Canada. Relationships, Loss, Happiness —
the songs document an entire mess of my experiences that all ended up on paper in a short period of time.



It’s a mistake to define music before listeners have a chance to hear it. They’re really just love songs — plain and simple.



052216 put the songs in context for me.
Dates heavily inform the way we write and consume. The date is important to me, and I really believe it will come across in the music."
- Joe Avio
October 4, 2018
